KeaBabies 2-Pack Bamboo Crib Sheets

Soft, breathable, and designed for comfort, these 2-pack crib sheets are made from 100% viscose derived from bamboo. Ideal for your baby or toddler, they provide a gentle touch on sensitive skin while maintaining a snug, secure fit with our innovative SnugFit Tech fabric knit.

Each sheet features a durable elastic band hidden under the seams, ensuring a seamless and lasting fit even as your child grows. The fitted crib mattress cover is machine washable and dryer friendly, making it easy to keep clean and fresh. Whether you're setting up a new nursery or updating your current one, these crib sheets are a perfect blend of style and practicality.

Why Choose KeaBabies Bamboo Crib Sheets?

  • Softness & Breathability: Made from naturally derived bamboo viscose, these crib sheets are incredibly soft and breathable, helping to keep your baby cool and comfortable throughout the day.

  • Sturdy Elastic Band: A high-quality, durable elastic band ensures a snug fit and adapts to different crib mattress dimensions, offering a safe and secure sleeping environment.
  • Machine Washable & Dryer Friendly: These sheets are built to last with easy care features, so you can keep them fresh without worrying about damage.
  • Neutral Meadow Print: A subtle and elegant design that complements any nursery decor, styled for both boys and girls.
